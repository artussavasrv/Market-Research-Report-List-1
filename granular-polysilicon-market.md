<p><strong>The Granular Polysilicon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SilaneMethod</li><li>MetallurgicalMethod</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The granular polysilicon market is primarily segmented into two types: the Silane Method and the Metallurgical Method. The Silane Method involves the chemical vapor deposition of silane gas to produce high-purity polysilicon, commonly used in semiconductor and solar industries. In contrast, the Metallurgical Method utilizes silicon produced from quartz and carbon, undergoing a purification process to create lower-cost polysilicon suitable for solar applications. Each method caters to different purity requirements and cost structures, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
