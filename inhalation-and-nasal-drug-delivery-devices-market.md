<p><p>The inhalation and nasal drug delivery devices market includes several types, each catering to different therapeutic needs. Metered-dose inhalers (MDIs) deliver precise doses of medication in aerosol form, while dry powder inhalers (DPIs) release medication as a fine powder activated by inhalation. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into mist for easier inhalation. Nasal sprays offer localized treatment for conditions like allergies, delivering medication directly through the nasal passages. Nasal inhalers provide portable, user-friendly options for systemic or localized drug delivery.</p></p>
