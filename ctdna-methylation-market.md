<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The ctDNA Methylation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Based on Restriction Enzyme</li><li>Based on Affinity Enrichment</li><li>Based on Bisulfite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ctDNA methylation market can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Based on Restriction Enzyme**: This method relies on specific enzymes to cut DNA at methylation-sensitive sites, enabling the analysis of methylation patterns.</p><p>2. **Based on Affinity Enrichment**: In this approach, methylated DNA is selectively captured using binding agents, enhancing the detection of methylation in low-abundance ctDNA.</p><p>3. **Based on Bisulfite Treatment**: This technique involves converting unmethylated cytosines to uracil, allowing for differential detection of methylated versus unmethylated DNA sequences, aiding in detailed methylation profiling.</p></p>
