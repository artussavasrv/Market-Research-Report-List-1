<p><strong>The Ground-source Heat Pump System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Underground Tube Ground Source Heat Pump System</li><li>Groundwater Source Heat Pump System</li><li>Surface Water Ground Source Heat Pump System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ground-source heat pump system market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Underground Tube Ground Source Heat Pump System** utilizes buried pipes filled with fluid to absorb heat from the ground, providing efficient heating and cooling. </p><p>2. **Groundwater Source Heat Pump System** extracts heat from groundwater through wells, offering a reliable temperature source.</p><p>3. **Surface Water Ground Source Heat Pump System** draws energy from nearby bodies of water, leveraging stable temperatures for efficient thermal regulation. These systems are essential for sustainable energy solutions.</p></p>
