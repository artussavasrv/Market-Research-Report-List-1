<p><strong>The Residential Boiler Heating System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Boilers</li><li>Hot Water Boilers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential boiler heating system market comprises primarily two types: steam boilers and hot water boilers. Steam boilers generate steam through heated water, providing efficient heating, particularly in older homes. They offer quick heat but require more maintenance. In contrast, hot water boilers circulate heated water through radiators or underfloor systems, providing consistent warmth and improved energy efficiency. Both systems cater to residential heating needs, with varying efficiency levels, installation costs, and operational characteristics tailored to homeowner preferences and infrastructure.</p></p>
