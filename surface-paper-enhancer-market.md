<p><p>The Surface Paper Enhancer market primarily includes two types: Wet Strength Agents and Dry Strength Agents. Wet Strength Agents are additives that improve the paper's resistance to water, enhancing durability during processing and end-use applications. In contrast, Dry Strength Agents enhance the tensile strength of paper in its dry state, ensuring better performance and reducing the likelihood of breakage during handling. Both agents play crucial roles in improving paper quality and functionality, catering to various demands in the paper industry.</p></p>
