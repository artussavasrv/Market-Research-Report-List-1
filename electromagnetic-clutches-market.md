<p><p>The electromagnetic clutches market can be categorized into four main types: dry, wet, magnetic powder, and others. Dry clutches operate without lubricants, utilizing friction to engage and disengage, ideal for lightweight applications. Wet clutches are submerged in oil, providing better cooling and durability, commonly used in high-power applications. Magnetic powder clutches utilize magnetizable powder to transfer torque, offering smooth engagement and precise control. Other types may include configurations like hysteresis or electromagnetic brakes, catering to specialized or niche applications.</p></p>
