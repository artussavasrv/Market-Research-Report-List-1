<p><strong>The Toxoplasmosis Diagnostic Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Immune Fluorescent Test (IFAT)</li><li>Modified Agglutination Test (MAT)</li><li>Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Immunoglobulin Tests</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Toxoplasmosis diagnostic testing market includes several key types. The Indirect Immune Fluorescent Test (IFAT) detects antibodies using fluorescent dyes, providing a sensitive assessment of infection. The Modified Agglutination Test (MAT) involves mixing patient serum with Toxoplasma antigens to identify antibody presence. The Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) quantifies specific antibodies, offering high specificity and ease of use. Immunoglobulin tests measure IgG and IgM antibodies to differentiate between acute and chronic infections, aiding in accurate diagnosis and management of Toxoplasmosis.</p></p>
