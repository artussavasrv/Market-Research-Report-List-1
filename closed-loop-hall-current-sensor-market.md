<p><p>The Closed Loop Hall Current Sensor market is divided into two main types: DC Hall Current Sensors and AC Hall Current Sensors. DC Hall Current Sensors are designed to accurately measure direct current by utilizing the Hall effect in a closed loop configuration, providing high precision and low offset errors. In contrast, AC Hall Current Sensors measure alternating current, offering real-time response and the ability to handle higher frequencies. Both types are essential for various applications in industrial automation, electric vehicles, and renewable energy systems.</p></p>
