<p><p>The intranasal drug delivery system encompasses various formulations designed to administer medication through the nasal cavity. Nasal drops are liquid solutions that are instilled directly into the nostrils, providing localized or systemic effects. Nasal sprays deliver a fine mist for rapid absorption. Nasal gels offer prolonged contact and reduced drainage. Nasal suspensions and emulsions combine solid particles or liquid droplets in a carrier for enhanced delivery. Nasal powders are inhaled as dry formulations, allowing for quick absorption and onset of action.</p></p>
