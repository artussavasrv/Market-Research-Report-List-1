<p><p>The hospital casting and splinting products market is categorized into two primary types: casting and splinting. Casting products, typically made of plaster or fiberglass, are used to immobilize broken bones and support healing. They provide full circumferential coverage for effective stabilization. Splinting products, on the other hand, offer support for injuries that require less immobilization, such as sprains or fractures that are not completely broken. Both types ensure proper alignment and protection during the recovery process.</p></p>
