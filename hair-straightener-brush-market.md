<p><p>The hair straightener brush market is primarily categorized into two types: ionic and non-ionic hair straightener brushes. Ionic hair straightener brushes utilize negative ions to reduce frizz and static, promoting smooth and shiny hair during styling. In contrast, non-ionic brushes rely on traditional heating methods without the ion technology, offering simpler, more basic straightening capabilities. These distinctions cater to varying consumer preferences, with ionic brushes often favored for their advanced features and efficiency in achieving a polished look.</p></p>
