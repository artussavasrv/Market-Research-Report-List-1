<p><p>The dental zirconia disc market is categorized into three types based on transparency: high, medium, and low transparency. High transparency discs are often used for anterior restorations, offering optimal aesthetics by mimicking natural tooth appearance. Medium transparency discs balance aesthetics and strength, suitable for both anterior and posterior applications. Low transparency discs are primarily utilized for posterior restorations, prioritizing durability over aesthetics. Each type serves specific dental needs, catering to varying patient preferences and clinical requirements in restorative dentistry.</p></p>
