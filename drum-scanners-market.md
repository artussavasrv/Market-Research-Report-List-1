<p><strong>The Drum Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD scanner</li><li>PMT sanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drum scanners are specialized devices for digitizing high-quality images, primarily used in professional settings like graphic design and printing. The two main market types are CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and PMT (Photomultiplier Tube) scanners. CCD scanners utilize electronic sensors to capture images quickly and efficiently, making them suitable for a range of applications. In contrast, PMT scanners employ photomultiplier tubes for higher sensitivity and superior color accuracy, making them ideal for intricate tasks that demand exceptional image quality.</p></p>
