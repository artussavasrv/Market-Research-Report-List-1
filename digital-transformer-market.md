<p><p>The digital transformer market is primarily segmented into two types: dry type and oil immersed type. Dry type transformers utilize air as the cooling medium, making them safer and environmentally friendly, ideal for indoor applications. In contrast, oil immersed transformers use transformer oil for cooling and insulation, offering higher efficiency and capacity, suitable for outdoor installations. Both types serve critical roles in energy distribution, with varying benefits tailored to specific operational requirements and installation environments.</p></p>
