<p><ul><li>SPECT</li><li>Hybrid PET</li><li>Planar Scintigraphy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nuclear imaging equipment encompasses several technologies, primarily SPECT, hybrid PET, and planar scintigraphy. SPECT (Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography) provides detailed 3D images of functional processes within the body, aiding in diagnosing various conditions. Hybrid PET (Positron Emission Tomography) combines PET with CT or MRI, enhancing anatomical localization of diseases. Planar scintigraphy offers 2D images and is simpler and less expensive, often used for specific evaluations like bone scans. Each type serves distinct clinical applications in medical diagnostics.</p></p>
