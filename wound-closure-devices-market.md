<p><p>The wound closure devices market includes various types designed to facilitate the healing process. Closure strips are adhesive strips used to hold skin edges together after surgery or injury, providing support without sutures. Tissue adhesives are surgical glues that bond tissue together, often applied in minimally invasive procedures for quick closure. Sutures involve stitching the wound edges, offering strong support and are commonly used in surgical settings. Each method has unique benefits, catering to different clinical requirements and patient needs.</p></p>
