<p><p>The solid-state connector market consists of various types, including receptacles, plugs, wire-to-wire connectors, and wire-to-board connectors. Receptacles serve as fixed sockets, securing connections with plugs. Plugs are connectors that insert into receptacles to create a circuit. Wire-to-wire connectors link two separate wires, facilitating electronic interactions without needing a PCB. Wire-to-board connectors connect wires directly to a circuit board, establishing a secure path for signals and power, essential for compact electronic device designs.</p></p>
