<p><ul><li>Single layer</li><li>Multilayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HDF laminate flooring is categorized into single-layer and multilayer options. Single-layer laminate features a single board design, typically offering a straightforward installation and a cost-effective solution. In contrast, multilayer laminate consists of multiple layers, including a high-density fiberboard core that enhances durability, sound insulation, and realism in appearance. This multilayer construction allows for improved resistance to wear and moisture, making it suitable for a wider range of applications. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
