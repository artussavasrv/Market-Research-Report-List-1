<p><strong>The Automotive Seat Tracks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Locking Tracks</li><li>Passive Non-Locking Tracks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive seat tracks market is categorized into active locking tracks and passive non-locking tracks. Active locking tracks utilize a mechanism that engages automatically to secure the seat position, enhancing safety and ease of adjustment. In contrast, passive non-locking tracks allow manual seat adjustments but lack an automatic locking feature, offering simpler mechanics. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with active locking tracks often preferred for safety-conscious models, while passive tracks are typically used in cost-effective vehicles.</p></p>
