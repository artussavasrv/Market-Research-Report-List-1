<p><p>The small size cooling fan market primarily consists of axial fans, centrifugal fans, and other specialized types. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, making them ideal for applications where space is limited. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, draw air into the fan and expel it at a right angle, providing higher pressure and airflow efficiency. Other fans may include specialty designs suited for unique requirements, such as vibration resistance or low noise operation, further diversifying the market.</p></p>
