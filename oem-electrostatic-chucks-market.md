<p><p>The OEM electrostatic chucks market is primarily categorized into two types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type chucks utilize electrostatic forces to hold substrates in place, offering high stability and precision, making them ideal for various semiconductor applications. In contrast, JR Type chucks leverage a combination of electrostatic and mechanical forces, providing enhanced grip and alignment accuracy, particularly in high-temperature environments. Both types play crucial roles in improving processes in semiconductor fabrication and other advanced manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
