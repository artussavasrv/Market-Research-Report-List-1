<p><p>The superhard cutting tool market includes two primary types: Polycrystalline Cubic Boron Nitride (PCBN) and Polycrystalline Diamond (PCD). PCBN tools are known for their exceptional hardness and thermal stability, making them ideal for machining hard materials like steel and superalloys. PCD tools, on the other hand, are highly effective for cutting non-ferrous materials, such as aluminum and composites, due to their superior wear resistance. Both types enhance machining efficiency and precision, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
