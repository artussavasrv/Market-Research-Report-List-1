<p><p>Yttrium target markets are classified into two main types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat surfaces used primarily in sputtering applications for thin film deposition, providing uniform coating over a substrate. In contrast, rotating targets are cylindrical and enable continuous material deposition, enhancing efficiency and uniformity in applications like semiconductor manufacturing. Both target types cater to specific industry needs, with plane targets being ideal for smaller batches and rotating targets suited for larger production runs and high-demand applications.</p></p>
