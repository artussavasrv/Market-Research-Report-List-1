<p><p>The portable battery market comprises several key types, each with distinct characteristics. Lithium-ion batteries are popular for their high energy density and light weight, making them ideal for smartphones and laptops. Lithium-polymer batteries offer flexibility in shape and are often used in compact devices. Nickel-cadmium batteries are known for durability and are typically used in power tools, while nickel-metal hydride batteries provide a balance of capacity and cost, commonly found in hybrid vehicles and consumer electronics.</p></p>
