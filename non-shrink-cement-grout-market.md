<p><p>The Non-shrink Cement Grout market is categorized into two main types: dry-packed and fluid packaged. Dry-packed grout consists of a coarse mixture that requires water addition before application, offering high strength for vertical or horizontal void filling. In contrast, fluid packaged grout is a pre-mixed formulation that is ready for immediate use, designed for ease of application in various construction settings due to its optimal flowability. Both types are essential for ensuring stability and durability in construction and repair projects.</p></p>
