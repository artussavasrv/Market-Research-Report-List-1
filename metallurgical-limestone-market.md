<p><ul><li>Dolomitic Limestone</li><li>Calcitic Dolomite</li><li>Magnesian Limestones</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallurgical limestone market comprises several types, including dolomitic limestone, calcitic dolomite, and magnesian limestone. Dolomitic limestone features a mix of calcium and magnesium carbonates, ideal for steelmaking and flux applications. Calcitic dolomite primarily contains calcium carbonate, often used in lime production. Magnesian limestone, rich in magnesium content, serves similar industrial purposes. Each type plays a crucial role in metallurgical processes, impacting steel quality and performance, thereby shaping demand within the global market.</p></p>
