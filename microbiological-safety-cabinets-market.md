<p><strong>The Microbiological Safety Cabinets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class II Microbiological Safety Cabinets</li><li>Class I Microbiological Safety Cabinets</li><li>Class III Microbiological Safety Cabinets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Microbiological Safety Cabinets market is classified into three primary types: Class I, Class II, and Class III cabinets. Class I cabinets provide personnel protection by using a unidirectional airflow to filter aerosols from the laboratory environment, but do not offer product protection. Class II cabinets protect personnel, products, and the environment through laminar airflow and HEPA filtration. Class III cabinets are gas-tight, providing the highest level of containment for dangerous pathogens, designed for work with highly hazardous agents.</p></p>
