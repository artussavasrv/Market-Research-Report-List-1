<p><p>The superalloy master alloys market is categorized into three primary types: iron-based, nickel-based, and cobalt-based superalloys. Iron-based superalloys are known for their cost-effectiveness and high mechanical strength, making them suitable for various applications. Nickel-based superalloys excel in high-temperature operations, offering exceptional resistance to oxidation and creep, widely used in aerospace and gas turbines. Cobalt-based superalloys provide superior wear resistance and thermal stability, often utilized in demanding environments like medical devices and high-performance engines. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
