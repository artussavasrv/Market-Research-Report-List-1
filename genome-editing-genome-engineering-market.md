<p><p>Genome editing and engineering market involves technologies like CRISPR, TALEN, and ZFN, each enabling precise modifications of an organism's DNA. CRISPR (Clustered Regularly Interspaced Short Palindromic Repeats) utilizes RNA guidance for targeted edits, offering simplicity and efficiency. TALEN ( transcription activator-like effector nuclease) employs engineered proteins for DNA recognition and alteration, while ZFN (zinc finger nuclease) uses zinc-finger proteins for site-specific cutting. Together, these technologies drive advancements in genetics, therapeutics, and agricultural applications, shaping the future of biological research.</p></p>
