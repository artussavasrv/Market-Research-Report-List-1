<p><p>The internal floating roof market features key types, including metal disc inner floating roofs and metal partition internal floating roofs. Metal disc inner floating roofs consist of circular metallic structures that float on the liquid surface, reducing evaporation and product loss. Both types enhance safety and efficiency in storage tanks by minimizing emissions and protecting the stored liquid from contamination.</p></p>
